# Healthcare-Analysis-Dashboard

📌 Project Overview
The Healthcare Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to monitor hospital billing, treatment costs, insurance coverage, patient demographics, and medical procedures. The dashboard provides healthcare administrators and decision-makers with actionable insights to improve operational efficiency and financial performance.

🎯 Business Objectives
Analyze overall healthcare billing and revenue.
Monitor treatment, room, and medication costs.
Track insurance-covered expenses versus out-of-pocket payments.
Evaluate department-wise financial performance.
Identify the most frequently used medical procedures.
Understand patient demographics and service utilization patterns.

💡 Key Insights Generated
Insurance covers a significant portion of total healthcare expenses.
Cardiology and Orthopedics contribute the highest billing amounts.
X-Ray procedures generate the highest billing revenue.
Outpatient services account for a large share of patient interactions.
Billing performance varies across geographic locations.
Patient distribution is nearly balanced across genders.
